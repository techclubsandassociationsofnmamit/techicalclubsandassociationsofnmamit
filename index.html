Technical Clubs of NMAMIT

Hackerearth Hub-Nmamit

Our club, Hackerearth Hub-Nmamit, enlightens and inspires all our fellow club members about Competitive Programming and its potential in the future. With the support of Hackerearth India, we host several weekly as well as monthly contests, fostering a healthy competitive environment when it comes to Competitive Programming.
Visit our website for more information or reach out to us at hackerearth.nmamit@nitte.edu.in.

Finite Loop Club

Finite Loop Club is one of the leading coding clubs in NMAMIT Campus. It is well known for organizing Technical Events such as Hackathons, CTF, Debates, Sprints and many more interesting activities that help the students interact with each other and form a community. Learning through Peers is the main ideology of FLC which helps the members to grow both as an individual and also as a team.
Visit their website for more information.
