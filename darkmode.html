Aeroclub Nitte

Aeroclub Nitte, established in 2013, has evolved beyond its origins as a student-led technical club into a thriving community united by a shared passion for conquering the skies. What began as a focus on analyzing, fabricating, and testing self-built UAVs has grown into a hub of excellence, showcasing a commitment to cutting-edge technology and aeronautical engineering. The club's core activities center around crafting elegant and efficient aircraft, with a particular specialization in aerodynamics. Their expertise in this field has been acknowledged in prestigious competitions such as the SAE Aero Design Competition and the National Level competition hosted by Boeing in IITs nationwide, where they proudly hold the SAE rank 1 south zone in 2023 in the micro class. Beyond technical achievements, Aeroclub Nitte prides itself on nurturing a vibrant aviation community within NMAM Institute of Technology (NMAMIT) and globally. They aim not only to innovate but also to share knowledge and skills, fostering an environment where members can excel and inspire one another. As the official aeromodelling team of NMAMIT, the club represents the institution's innovative spirit on a global stage, showcasing engineering creativity and dedication to aeronautical excellence. With a vision to become a leading force in drone technology and innovation, Aeroclub Nitte envisions fostering technical excellence, building a vibrant aviation community, and expanding its global influence through various initiatives such as RC aeromodelling workshops, outreach programs, and collaborations. Their mission is clear: to inspire and propel members to achieve remarkable success in the aviation realm while maintaining a steadfast commitment to excellence.

Visit their website or reach out to us at aeroclubnitte@nmamit.in
